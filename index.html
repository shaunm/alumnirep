About Me

Hi! My name is Shaun Maher, a rising junior at Columbia College originally from New York. I am studying Applied Math and Computer Science. I am honored to be a member of our campus' rich community. You can catch me on campus doing work at Dear Mama or sitting outside in front of Hartley. I enjoy connecting with the broader New York City community and taking advantage of our city's cultural and historic pillars. I hope to be your next Alumni Affairs Representative and enhance and help all of us build strong and lasting relationships with previous Lions.

My Platform

  • Fostering a More Inclusive Alumni Environment: If given the privilege to serve as your Alumni Affairs Representative, I will bridge the gap between Columbia College's Student Council and the College's Alumni Association and better represent traditionally underrepresented alumni from our school. I will fight to create events that highlight these members and increase opportunities to connect with them.
  • Developing Rich In-Person Experiences: As we enter the later stages of the pandemic, many of us have become accustomed to Zoom-only events. While convenient, these digital experiences lack much of the dimension gained from being in-person. If elected, I will work tirelessly to create events that facilitate networking, advice sharing, and other out-of-the-box panels that have a safe and exciting in-person option.
  • Creating More Personal Alumni Opportunities: Panels are essential to gaining valuable wisdom from alumni in our field of focus and others, but sometimes these events don't yield the personal outcomes that many students might hope. Smaller, more intimate events tailored to one's interests offer a viable way for students and alumni to build more meaningful, long-term relationships. If elected to be your Alumni Affairs Representative, I will respond to the student body's desires and work to implement more tailored events.
